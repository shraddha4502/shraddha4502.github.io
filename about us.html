ABOUT US

Our Story

The Brew Box was founded in 2009 with the vision to foster a community that connects local artists, musicians and nomads. We provide a space for self-expression and individuality centered around ethically sourced coffee and playful food.

Our Vision

We at The Brew Box want to be a space that uplifts the voices that have been silenced for so many years. We stand in solidarity with the fight against systemic racism, white supremacy, police brutality, and the historic oppression of the Black community. Black Lives Matter.
